1.1 Introduction of Numbers

Numbers are fundamental mathematical objects used for counting, measuring, and labeling. Natural numbers are denoted by N.

1.3 Whole Numbers

Whole numbers are natural numbers plus zero. Examples: 0, 1, 2, 3, …

1.4 Rational Numbers

A rational number is written as p/q where q ≠ 0.
